## Description

Automate the merchant onboarding flow end-to-end: KYB verification, risk assessment, contract generation, and account provisioning. Before this initiative, onboarding took an average of 8.3 business days and required 14 manual touchpoints from our operations team. For a company targeting 3,200 active merchants by Q4, that process doesn't scale.

We integrated Middesk for automated KYB, built a risk scoring model using merchant application data, automated contract generation via PandaDoc API, and created a self-serve provisioning flow that spins up merchant accounts with appropriate rate plans and risk limits.

## What Shipped

1. **Automated KYB** (Middesk integration): Business verification completes in < 4 hours for 89% of applications
2. **Risk Scoring Model**: Logistic regression model scores merchant risk (low/medium/high) based on 23 features; low-risk merchants auto-approve
3. **Contract Automation** (PandaDoc): Contracts auto-generated with correct rate plans, sent for e-signature within 1 hour of approval
4. **Self-Serve Provisioning**: Approved merchants get API keys, sandbox environment, and onboarding guide automatically
5. **Operations Dashboard**: Real-time view of onboarding pipeline for manual review of medium/high-risk applications

## Retrospective

**What went well**: Middesk integration was cleaner than expected. Risk scoring model accuracy exceeded targets. Operations team adoption was immediate — they had been asking for this for 6 months.

**What we'd do differently**: Should have involved legal earlier — contract template review added 5 days to the timeline. PandaDoc API rate limits caused issues in load testing that we didn't catch until week 3.

**Impact after 2 weeks live**: 43 merchants onboarded through the new flow. Average time: 1.4 business days. Zero escalations. Operations team reallocated 34 hours/week to merchant success activities.
