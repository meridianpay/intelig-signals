# Meridian Pay — Product Vision

> Last updated: 2026-02-14 | Owner: Sarah Kim, VP Product | Approved by: Marcus Chen, CEO

## Vision Statement

**Become the default payment infrastructure for mid-market SaaS companies by 2027.**

Meridian Pay exists to eliminate the payment complexity tax that mid-market SaaS companies pay when they outgrow Stripe but can't justify building in-house. We provide the reliability of enterprise payment infrastructure with the developer experience of modern fintech — at a price point that scales with our customers.

By the end of 2027, every mid-market SaaS company evaluating payment infrastructure should have Meridian Pay on their shortlist. We win by being the platform that engineering teams _want_ to integrate and finance teams _trust_ to run.

## Strategic Pillars

### 1. Revenue Acceleration
Unlock new revenue streams for our merchants and for Meridian. Multi-currency support, enterprise billing features, and geographic expansion are the growth levers. Our merchants process $2.1B annually today — we're targeting $4.5B by EOY 2027.

### 2. Platform Resilience
Decompose the monolith. Hit 99.99% uptime. Pass PCI DSS 4.0 without a single finding. Our infrastructure is the foundation everything else is built on — if it's fragile, nothing else matters. Zero tolerance for payment processing outages.

### 3. Developer Experience
SDK v3, a rebuilt merchant portal, and API improvements that make integration take hours, not weeks. Our NPS among integrating developers is 38 today — target is 65+ by Q4 2026. The best acquisition channel is an engineer who had a great experience.

### 4. Intelligent Operations
AI-powered fraud detection, automated merchant onboarding, and predictive analytics. Reduce manual review rates from 4.2% to under 1%. Turn operational cost centers into competitive advantages.
